pure curum

Description

This is an easy guide to obtain pure curum

Ingredients

  • purified fleeb
  • mutant bacteria cell
  • supercharged battery

Directions

  1. mix in fleeb in the supercharged battery juice
  2. hook electrodes up from battery to the bacteria cell
  3. when the cell turns to mush, collect this gel
  4. run gel through one cycle of the discombobulator
  5. you're done!
