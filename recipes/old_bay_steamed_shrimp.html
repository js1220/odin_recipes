Old Bay Steamed Shrimp

Description

This is an easy to make steamed shrimp recipe that should only take about 20 min to make!

Ingredients

  • Raw Shrimp
  • Old Bay Seasoning
  • Butter

Directions

  1. Bring water, vinegar, and seafood seasoning to a boil in a saucepan over high heat
  2. Add shrimp and stir
  3. Reduce heat to medium, cover, and steam, stirring once or twice, until bright orange in color, 3 to 5 minutes
  4. Drain
