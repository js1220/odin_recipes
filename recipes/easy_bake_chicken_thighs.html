Easy Bake Chicken Thighs

Description

This is an easy to make baked chicken thighs recipe that should only take about 45min!

Ingredients

  • Chicken Thighs (raw)
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt/ Pepper
  • Onion Powder
  • Garlic Powder

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 410
  2. Put chicken into pan
  3. Add in 3 tbls olive oil
  4. Add in dusting of each spice
  5. Bake for 35 min
